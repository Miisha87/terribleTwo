Contact

Are you in need of support and contact?
Here are some ways for you to come in contact with others who might be supportive for you in this time of crisis.




SOS ALARM

If you find your twoyearold terrorist jumping from roofs, hugging crocodiles or eating scissors, knifes or even the household cat.

CALL 911
ON-DUTY-PRIEST

Sometimes, it can be neccessary to call an on-duty priest. Either to perform exorcism, or just to have someone bringing wine and biscuits while giving you some hope that things will be better.

Find a priest


ON-DUTY-FELLOWS

Believe it or not, there are parents who have survived this and actually came out somehow stronger on the other side. Some of these people are now working voluntarily with supporting other parents online.

Contact a fellow
